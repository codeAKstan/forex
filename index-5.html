How can I invest with Forex Crypto Automated System ?
To make a investment you must first become a member of Forex Crypto Automated System . Once you are signed up, you can make your first deposit. All deposits must be made through the Members Area. You can login using the member username and password you receive when signup.
How do I open my Forex Crypto Automated System Account?
It's quite easy and convenient. Follow this link, fill in the registration form and then press "Register".
Which e-currencies do you accept?
We accept e-currencies.
How long does it take for my deposit to be added to my account?
Your account will be updated as fast, as you deposit.
How can I change my e-mail address or password?
Log into your Forex Crypto Automated System account and click on the "Account Information". You can change your e-mail address and password there.
What if I can't log into my account because I forgot my password?
Click forgot password link, type your username or e-mail and you'll receive your account information.
Does a hourly profit paid directly to my currency account?
No, profits are gathered on your Forex Crypto Automated System account and you can withdraw them anytime.
How do you calculate the interest on my account?
Depending on each plan. Interest on your Forex Crypto Automated System account is acquired hourly credited to your available balance at the end of each hour.
Can I do a direct deposit from my account balance?
Yes! To make a deposit from your Forex Crypto Automated System account balance. Simply login into your members account and click on Make Deposit ans select the Deposit from Account Balance Radio button.
Can I make an additional deposit to Forex Crypto Automated System account has been opened?
Yes, you can but all transactions are handled separately.
Will you add more payment processors?
Yes, we will consider to add Advcash, Bitcoin cash, Degocoin, Dash, Eos etc when the project is more stable.
What price do you use for Bitcoin，Ethereum and Litecoin?
We use bitstamp.net price for Bitcoin, Ethereum and Litecoin when you deposit or withdraw.
When the deposit should be activated?
PerfectMoney and Payeer deposits are credited to your account instantly. Bitcoin,Ethereum,Litecoin deposits will be credit to your account after 3 confirmation.
May I create a deposit from my account balance?
Yes. In order to make a deposit from your account balance you need to select 'Spend funds from account balance' on 'Make Invest'page in your Member area.
May I have several deposits at the same time?
Yes, sure you may have as many deposits as you want.

How fast will my withdrawal be processed?
Your request will be processed automatically We will do everything possible to reduce awaiting time of our clients.
